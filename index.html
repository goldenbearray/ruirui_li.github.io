Welcome to Ruirui Li's homepage!

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science at UCLA, supervised by Prof. Wei Wang. I received my B.S. degree from Department of Computer Science & Technology, Nanjing University in June 2010. After that, I got a master degree from the department of computer science, the university of Hong Kong in June 2012.
